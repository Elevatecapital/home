Crowd Funding

Property investors can invest in property from as little as £10.00 and still benefit from the returns of owning the property. There are a number of ways investors can utilise crowd funding either becoming a investor in other properties looking to raise finance, raise finance through peer to peer lending or put forward a property to be crowd funded, if you have sourced a good opportunity crowd funding companies will pay you a fee.

Development Finance

This can be for a number of projects including refurbishments, extending, converting or new build, generally the development finance is over a short term period between 6 – 18 months depending on the size of the project.
